Cancellation and Refund Policy

Welcome to EazzyCollab.com! We strive to ensure a fair and smooth collaboration experience for both brands and influencers. Please read our cancellation and refund policy carefully.

Payment Flow:

  • Payment Initiation: When a brand books a collaboration with an influencer, the payment is made and held securely by EazzyCollab.com.
  • Work Completion: The influencer completes the agreed-upon work and submits it for review.
  • Approval Process: The brand reviews the work. If the brand is satisfied with the work, they approve the payment.
  • Payment Release: Once the brand approves the work, the payment is released to the influencer.

Refund Policy:

  • No Refunds: Refunds are generally not allowed once the payment is initiated.
  • Exceptional Cases: Refunds are only considered in genuine cases, such as:
    • Non-delivery or late delivery of work by the influencer.
    • Mistaken payments.
  • Approval Required: If a refund is requested due to one of the exceptional cases, it must be approved by our support team.
  • Post-Approval: Once the payment is approved by the brand and released to the influencer, no refunds will be generated.

Additional Notes:

  • Payment Security: We hold the payment from the brand until the influencer completes the work and it is approved by the brand.
  • Dispute Resolution: In case of disputes, both parties can contact our support team to resolve the issue.

By using EazzyCollab.com, both brands and influencers agree to adhere to this policy to ensure a fair and smooth collaboration experience.
