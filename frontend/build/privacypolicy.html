Privacy Policy for EazzyCollab

Welcome to EazzyCollab. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is secure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit and use our website.

Information We Collect

User Categories

EazzyCollab categorizes users into two groups: Brands and Influencers.

For All Users

  • Email Address: Collected for login and account creation.

For Influencers

  • Name: Collected for profile creation.
  • Social Media Profiles: Collected to enhance personalization and facilitate collaboration with Brands.

Use of Collected Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

Payment Information

Payments on EazzyCollab are processed through Razorpay. We do not collect or store any payment information. Razorpay handles all payment processing securely.

Cookies

EazzyCollab uses cookies to enhance user experience and personalization. Cookies help us understand user preferences and improve our services.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with any third parties. Your data is used solely for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
